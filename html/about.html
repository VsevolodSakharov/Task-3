About our app

"My Bookshelf" is a simple web-application which allows you to browse books, find out more about them and add those that you're interested in into your own virtual bookshelf. If you want to get started, simply go over to the search panel through navigation menu located at the top-right side of the page. There you will be able to find any book you'd like by selecting specific search options and entering your prompt.
